# CohnReznick

CohnReznick is a top-25 US accounting, tax, and advisory firm formed in 2012 by the merger of J.H. Cohn LLP and Reznick Group, P.C. The firm serves clients across more than 30 industries from offices throughout the United States and four offshore subsidiaries, with its global reach extended through membership in the Nexia International network ("more than 128 countries"). In May 2024 CohnReznick became the first US accounting firm of its scale to receive a strategic growth investment from a private equity sponsor when funds advised by Apax Partners acquired a majority stake.

CohnReznick does not publish a public software developer API. This repository documents the firm's organizational profile, practice taxonomy, and linked-data context for API catalog purposes.

## Alternative Practice Structure

The May 2024 Apax investment required CohnReznick to adopt an alternative-practice structure consistent with US state-board rules on auditor ownership:

- **CohnReznick LLP** — Licensed CPA firm that delivers attest, audit, and accounting services. Remains owned by its CPA partners.
- **CohnReznick Advisory LLC** — Delivers tax and business consulting services. Majority-owned by funds advised by Apax Partners.

This makes CohnReznick the first US accounting firm of its scale to operate under majority private-equity ownership.
